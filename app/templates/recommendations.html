Traditionally, recommendation engines need a lot of data from community members that are aggregated with your specific data to learn from. This allows the engine to make accurate recommendations of similarity to items of interest.

The “Green Thumb” AI recommendation engine fills the missing data gap, called the “cold start” problem, by surveying some plants we have in the database. Upon your selections, we summarize and aggregate the variables for each plant in the category of your sentiment for “add to cart,” “thumbs up” or “thumbs down”. For “add to cart” we add 2 points, and for “thumbs up” we add 1 point, and lastly for “thumbs down” we subtract 1 point.
